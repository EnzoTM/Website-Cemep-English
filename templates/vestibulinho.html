High school admission test 2021

Course offered: Technical school in informatic
Period: Integral (morning and afternoon classes)
Duration: 3 years
Places: 80 + 3 for people with disabilities

Application requirements

  • The candidate must be attending the freshman year during 2020
  • Candidates won't be able to do the application if they
    had finished freshman year before 2020




How to get in

  • This year in-person tests will not be applied, becuase of the pandemic
  • The criterion adopted will be the analysis of the 2019 School History

Application

  • The period runs until 10/31/2020
  • Where to register: www.paulinia.sp.gov.br




Criteria for 1st call

  • 40% (forty percent) of the vacancies are destined to candidates coming from
    the public network residing in Paulínia
  • 10% (ten percent) of the vacancies are destined to candidates from the
    public network residents of other municipalities
  • 40% (forty percent) of the vacancies are destined to candidates coming from
    private network resident in Paulínia
  • 10% (ten percent) of the vacancies are destined to candidates from thea
    private network residents of other municipalities

Criteria for 2st call

    From the second call the calls will obey the GENERAL CLASSIFICATION
    regardless of whether the candidates come from public schools or
    private, resident or not in Paulínia.
